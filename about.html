About Me

About Me

I am Zacc Munuhe, an undergraduate student at the University of Nairobi pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Microprocessor Technology, Controls and Instrumentation. My academic and technical focus lies at the intersection of embedded systems, artificial intelligence, and hardware-software integration.

I am driven by the need to understand how systems work at a fundamental level — from low-level hardware interactions to intelligent decision-making algorithms. Rather than only using technology, I aim to design and build systems that solve real-world problems efficiently and at scale.

Why Embedded Systems

I chose embedded systems because they form the backbone of modern technology. Every smart device — from mobile phones and medical equipment to industrial machines and IoT systems — relies on embedded systems for control and operation.

Unlike general software development, embedded systems require a deep understanding of both hardware and software. This dual nature makes the field more challenging and more impactful. It involves working with microcontrollers, real-time constraints, memory optimization, and direct hardware interfacing.

My interest specifically comes from:

  • The ability to build efficient systems with limited resources (memory, power, processing)
  • Direct interaction with physical hardware and electronics
  • Designing real-time systems that must be reliable and precise
  • Bridging the gap between electronics and intelligent software systems

In the long term, I aim to integrate embedded systems with artificial intelligence to build smart, autonomous, and adaptive technologies.

Technical Interests

  • Embedded Systems Development: Microcontrollers, firmware development, real-time systems
  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning: Applying AI to hardware-constrained environments
  • Programming: System-level programming using languages such as C ,Python and micro python and Rust
  • Electronics & Circuit Design: Understanding and building hardware systems
  • Control Systems & Instrumentation: Designing systems that monitor and regulate processes
  • Internet of Things (IoT): Connected devices and smart environments

Professional Direction

My goal is to build a career in the tech industry focused on developing intelligent systems that solve real-world problems. I am particularly interested in roles such as:

  • Embedded Systems Engineer
  • Firmware Developer
  • AI Systems Engineer (Edge AI)
  • Robotics and Automation Engineer
  • IoT Systems Developer

Beyond employment, I also aim to build and contribute to innovative tech solutions and eventually develop products or systems that address challenges in areas such as automation, healthcare, and smart infrastructure.

Learning Approach

I focus on practical, hands-on learning by building projects, experimenting with hardware, and continuously improving my programming and system design skills. I prioritize understanding concepts deeply rather than memorizing them, especially in areas such as signal processing, control systems, and embedded programming.

This website serves as a documentation of my journey — including projects, experiments, and technical progress — as I develop competence in embedded systems and AI.

Vision

My long-term vision is to contribute to the development of intelligent, efficient, and scalable technologies that can operate in real-world environments. I aim to be part of the next generation of engineers building systems that combine hardware precision with software intelligence.

I believe the future lies in the integration of embedded systems and artificial intelligence, and I am actively preparing myself to operate at that intersection.
