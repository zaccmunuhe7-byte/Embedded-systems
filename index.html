About Embedded Systems

Embedded systems are specialized computing systems designed to perform dedicated and often real-time functions within larger mechanical or electrical systems. Unlike general-purpose computers, embedded systems are optimized for efficiency, reliability, and precise control of hardware processes.

They are fundamental in modern technology, powering systems such as:

  • Consumer electronics (smart devices, wearables)
  • Automotive control systems (ECUs, sensors)
  • Industrial automation and robotics
  • Medical devices and monitoring systems
  • Internet of Things (IoT) applications

My focus is not only understanding these systems, but also designing and building them — from low-level firmware to complete functional prototypes.
